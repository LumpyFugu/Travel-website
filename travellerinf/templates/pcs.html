Wechat Pay

1. How to use Wechat pay?

（1）Download WeChat from the App Store.
（2）Complete registration, login and open up on your phone.
（3）Click “Me” on the bottom menu to find my “Wallet”,
where you can click on the “Cards” menu item to add your credit cards.

Alipay

1. How to use Alipay?

（1）Download the Alipay app onto your smartphone from the app store.
（2）It is free to sign up for an account with Alipay.
（3）You can then add and save your credit and debit card details within your account to start making mobile payments.
（4）If the remaining balance is sufficient, you can use the remaining balance in your Alipay account to pay.
（5）If you do not have enough balance in your Alipay account, you can choose to pay through online banking.

2. Shopping with Alipay

（1）One option is the store or vendor can scan your unique barcode.
（2）Alternatively, scan the barcode of the merchant when buying items or services from.

Common advantages of Wechat pay & Alipay:

（1）Safe and secure:

A pin code is needed to authorise every transaction. If you have a smartphone with touch ID feature, then you can also use your fingerprint to authorise the transaction.

（2）Fast and convenient:

Enter your pin code to make a payment online, no repetitive filling out of personal info and card details for every transaction.
