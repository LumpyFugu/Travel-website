Chinese Policy under COVID-19

Travel safety guarantee in China

No traveling to the provinces, municipalities or provincial level administrative regions with medium-risk and high-risk areas.

Must have:


(1) Green Health Codes (2) Green 14-Day Dynamic Trip Record

Suggest to have:

(1) Prepare enough masks (at least one per day per person) and wear a mask over your nose and mouth during your trip

(2) Hand sanitizer and wash your hands regularly

(3) A small bottle of disinfectant fluid or ethyl alcohol, disinfecting whips (better to buy at your travel destination in case of the restriction of flight or train security inspection)

(4) Try to use electronic payment to reduce contact

(ChinaDiscovery 2021)

China's measures to boost tourism during the COVID-19 pandemic

(1) Increase publicity online

Nearly 7,000 audio guided Tours including 3,000 scenic spots were available for free, and the number of visitors to the event increased by 600% in one week. Marketing is encouraged through the popular ways like live broadcast and short video, and more and more local government leaders, such as mayors, heads of departments and county heads, are also participating in online publicity. (Fronde 2021)

(2) Financial measures

Tax reduction: The value-added tax rate for small-scale taxpayers will be reduced from 3% to 1% in 2020. Refund of quality guarantee: 80% of the travel agency's quality guarantee is temporarily returned within two years; Loan guarantee: provide loan guarantee for accommodation and catering industry, and cultural and tourism industry, and cannot blindly withdraw loans, cut off loans, pressure loans; (Xie 2021)

(3) Transformation of tour mode

With the intelligent development of tourism, ‘unmanned service’, ‘virtual reality’ and ‘intelligent robot guide’ have become the basic requirements of major tourism enterprises and scenic areas. Cloud tourism a new tourism mode. (Wang 2020)
