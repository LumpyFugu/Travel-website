Guide for Travel Code

What is Travel Code?

Travel Code is a public welfare travel query service jointly launched by China Academy of information technology, China Telecom, China Mobile and China Unicom under the guidance of the Ministry of industry and information technology. It can provide users with certificates of countries (regions) and domestic cities that I have visited in the past 14 days and stayed for more than 4 hours free of charge. The data is relatively accurate and easy to use.

Main functions and processes

The communication travel card query service is mainly for Chinese mobile phone users. It can query individual domestic and international travel within the first 14 days. It is accurate to provinces and cities in China (stay for more than 4 hours) and countries abroad. The query is initiated by users, mobile phone software (WeChat, Alipay, etc.) can be used to scan the code access query page, or WeChat applet search "communication travel card" into the small program, enter the mobile phone number and click to get the verification code, verify the success and click on the query, then return to the query results.

A novel coronavirus pneumonia APP is added to the Bluetooth version of the communication calendar. If you are close to a stranger, you are diagnosed with new crown pneumonia diagnosed / suspected patients. Our service will remind you to observe isolation more timely.

Note:

1) New card users can only use the travel inquiry service after 14 days

2) For the time being, it is not supported to query the itinerary of cooperative operators with one card and multiple numbers, and the secondary number (for example, if the primary number is Hong Kong and Macao operators and the secondary number is inland operators)

Query result rules

The query results show that the visited provinces and cities (domestic), countries and regions (International) are divided into green, yellow, orange and red cards. The rules will be adjusted in real time according to the actual situation. The travel calculation of the user in the first 14 days is completed in the background of the operator, resulting in a one-day delay. For example, the travel data calculated on September 15 is the travel data in the 14 days before September 15 (between September 1 and September 14). In addition, since the service uses base station data, in order to ensure continuous communication and realize coverage without blind area, the signals of the two base stations at the junction of administrative divisions may cross cover, resulting in deviation of the results. When using, corresponding adjustments can be made in combination with other proofs.

The query result page will be marked with four colors: green, yellow, orange and red, and the rules will be adjusted in real time according to the actual situation.
