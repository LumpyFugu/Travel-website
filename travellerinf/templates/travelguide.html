Online travel in China

The background of "cloud tourism"

Offline tourism has been significantly impacted by COVID-19, but it has catalyzed the new idea of "cloud tourism" in the industry.

The definition of “cloud tourism”

"Cloud tourism" is a new tourism model that allows tourists to stay at home and enjoy the beautiful scenery. It is a way to frame tourism in the "cloud". It adopts information technology, cloud technology, mobile technology, artificial intelligence technology and so on, which enables tourists to travel through one or more end devices. In addition, by providing live-streaming, full-view web browsing, intelligent wearable experience and other forms, ravel consumers can realize various online tourism activities, such as sightseeing, experience, social interaction and shopping.







The benefits of “cloud tourism”（Xiao 2021）

1. Breaking through the time, space and money constraints

2. An effective measure to protect the world heritage

3. Drive online product sales

4. A way of sustainable development





Some applications

1. “The panoramic Palace Museum"

Through panoramic web browsing, visitors can have a sweeping view of travel guides and historical introductions of the Palace Museum.



2."Cloud Viewing cherry Blossom” in Wu Han University

5G unmanned camera vehicles patrol the Cherry Blossom Avenue of Wuhan University, collecting and transmitting live image signals in real time.



3. Visit museums along the Yellow River via live streaming events

The three-day broadcast of the museum attracted 15.93 million online viewers.
