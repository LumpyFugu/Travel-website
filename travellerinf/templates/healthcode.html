Q1: How to obtain "Application code"?

A: After becoming A real-name registered user of "Follow the Application" or "HealthCloud", Method 1: Open "Suibid" (APP, wechat mini program, Alipay mini program) or "Health Cloud" platform, and choose "SuiBid Code" to obtain. Method 2: Open wechat, Alipay or the "With the application" APP "Scan" function, scan the qr code posted below the code line, you can get the "with the application code".

Q2: How do people outside Shanghai obtain the "Application code"?

A: As long as the Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan and foreign nationals conduct real-name authentication in Alipay, they can get the "code with application" smoothly through the alipay mini program. People from Hong Kong and Macao can also obtain the "Application code with Application" directly by registering real-name users of the APP.

Q3: What should people outside Shanghai pay attention to in the process of obtaining the "Application code"?

A: People from Hong Kong and Macao can register and log in the "Alipay" APP through the Mainland Travel Permit number of Hong Kong and Macao Residents. Taiwan compatriots can register and log in the Alipay APP with the Mainland Travel Permit for Taiwan residents, and ensure that they have logged in the Alipay APP with their real names and entered the mini program to obtain the "Application code".

Q4: For foreigners, do you provide English version of the code with application?

A: Yes. At the same time, the English version of "I want to appeal" and faQs will be provided for foreigners to provide more convenient services.

Q5: There are a lot of my personal information in the "Application code", can you guarantee the data security?

A: The design architecture of Suishen Code relies on "One Network Tongdo", which runs in the E-government cloud of Shanghai. The data is distributed in the independent and controllable cloud platform. The security system is relatively strong and the data security is more guaranteed.
